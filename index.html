Welcome to the gates of Hell

Upon your arrival Ainz Ooal Gowns will drag you through the gates of hell and take you to where you will spend the rest of eternity. In hell there are two main places the Tower of Greed and Tower of Betryal, since everything can be placed in these two places.

Your Future

As you are dragged toward your worst nightmare, you see hundreds of these lifeless husks, bound by what seems to be red strings. These being, are said to be humans that have been granted "mercy" by the one named Heldeshi.

Sables

Once you arrive at your destination, you will be given to Sables the guide where he will give you an offer to escape from your punishment. He will present a way out of hell, but you better hope you do not fail this task.

Will you take the guides offer?

You might end up occurring all the wraith of hell in its entirety. Even in the afterlife, those consumed by greed must be punished. For if you fail, you become Satan's play things where he can and will do anything he wants to keep himself entertained.
